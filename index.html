Introduction

My research interests are analog ICs, ASIC design and analysis. My current field of interest is the development and implementation of LiDAR sensors. I worked as a researcher at GITC (Gyeongbuk IT Convergence Industry and Technology Institute) from 2015 to 2018, and worked on signal processing and circuit design for various sensors including LiDAR sensors. From 2018 to 2022, I was a senior researcher at CARNAVICOM Co., Ltd., where I designed the circuit design of the LiDAR sensor and developed the DCU. Currently, I am working as a professor for industry-academic cooperation, working on industry-academic cooperation at STAAR, Kyungpook National University.
