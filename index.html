Full Bio Sketch

My research interests include analog ICs, ASIC design and analysis. My current fields of interest is development and implementation of LiDAR sensor. I was a research engineer at GITC (Gyeongbuk of IT Convergence Industry Technology) from 2015 to 2018, and have worked on signal processing and circuit design for various sensors included LiDAR sensor. Since 2018, he is now a senior researcher in CARNAVICOM Co., Ltd., and working on circuit design of LiDAR sensor.
